# Product

## Register

product

## Users

JMC staff, administrators, students, employees, and library patrons use the system in administrative workstations, public kiosks, and self-service contexts. They need to complete attendance, circulation, catalog, patron, reporting, reservation, and account workflows quickly and confidently.

## Product Purpose

JMC combines school attendance and library management in one dependable system. It should make routine institutional work efficient, preserve clear boundaries between attendance and library data, and help users understand status, next actions, and outcomes without unnecessary complexity.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, efficient, and welcoming. The experience should feel calm and institutional while remaining approachable, with strong hierarchy, predictable shadcn/ui patterns, and task-focused workflows.

## Anti-references

Avoid flashy marketing-page treatments, dense legacy ERP screens, excessive gradients, glassmorphism, playful consumer-app styling, and inconsistent iconography. The interface should not trade clarity or familiarity for decorative novelty.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next task obvious through clear hierarchy and concise actions.
2. Prefer familiar, consistent interaction patterns over invented affordances.
3. Communicate status with redundant visual and textual cues, never color alone.
4. Keep high-frequency administrative workflows efficient without becoming visually dense.
5. Preserve a calm, welcoming institutional character across public and staff surfaces.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 Level AA. Support complete keyboard navigation, visible focus states, reduced-motion preferences, sufficient text and non-text contrast, color-blind-safe status indicators, and meaningful screen-reader semantics throughout staff, patron, and kiosk workflows.
